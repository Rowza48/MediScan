About MediScan

Learn about our mission, technology, and the team behind the project

Our Mission

MediScan was developed with the mission to make advanced medical diagnostics accessible to healthcare professionals worldwide. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, we aim to reduce diagnostic errors and improve patient outcomes.

Our Technology

Using state-of-the-art deep learning architectures including Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Transfer Learning, MediScan can accurately identify various lung conditions from chest X-ray images with precision matching expert radiologists.

Research Based

Our algorithms have been trained and validated on datasets containing over 100,000 chest X-ray images, ensuring robust performance across diverse patient demographics and imaging equipment.
